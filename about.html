About Us

You don’t live on processed and preserved food. Why should your pet?

Orgo Dog brings you natural, organic pet food, treats, and toiletries to your home, every month. Our customer is the busy pet parent who does not compromise eating and living healthy, for themselves and for their furry ones. And this commitment starts with eating fresh and organic. By using only pronounceable ingredients, preservative-free manufacturing, and quality nutritious protein in your pet’s food and treats, we help keep your kids on the right track to a long and healthy life, by delivering every month to your door.

You make the healthy choice, we take care of the rest.
